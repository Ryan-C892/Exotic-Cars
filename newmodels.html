New Exotic Car Models

2019 McLaren GT

The 2019 rendition of the McLaren boasts a 4.0 liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine with a torque in the range of 630Nm. The aerodynamic design combined with a light weight of 1,530kg gives this supercar a relaxing yet thrilling performance. Handling and stability giving the driver greater control and a smooth ride. Acceleration starts from 0 to 62 mph in just 3.2 seconds with a top speed of 203 mph making it the fastest McLaren on the market.

2016 Koenigsegg Regera

The Koenigsegg Regera sports a powerful twin-turbo V8 engine with three electric motors supported by a new powertrain technology called Koenigsegg Direct Drive. This makes the engine more compact while making the car lighter and faster. This supercar has electronically reached a top speed of 255 mph going from 0 to 62 mph in 2.8 seconds. The Regera also has more control and comfort due to a rear sub frame that allows the engine and transmission to rest on active soft mounts allowing the car to adjust to the driver's motions and reduce engine noise.

2019 Pagani Huayra Roadster BC

The Huayra BC Roadster is an open top variant of the Huarya BC. it had the same aerodynamic parts but with one exception: a powerful 6.0 liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine rated with 789 horsepower. Acceleration is crazy for such a strong engine going from 0 to 60 mph in 2.2 seconds with a top speed of 236 mph. It also has a lightweight single clutch transmission making the Huayra Roadster BC lighter and more nimble.

2019 Bugatti Centodieci

Bugatti's limited-edition supercar of 2019 is already impressive with it's sleak bodywork and luxury features. However, it's speed deserves mention. Powered by an 8.0 liter quad-turbocharged W-16 engine with 1577 horsepower and an acceleration of 2.4 seconds from 0 to 62 mph the Centodieci outdoes the Bugatti Chiron, another popular model, by 0.5 seconds.

2020 Tesla Roadster

Tesla hopes to take electric power to the fast lane with the 2020 Tesla Roadster. The company claims the car can go from 0 to 62mph in a staggering 1.9 seconds and a top speed of 250 mph making it faster than any legal civilian car to date. The bodywork is fittingly aerodynamic with an comfortable interior capable of seating four and featuring a removable glass roof that can be stored in the trunk.
